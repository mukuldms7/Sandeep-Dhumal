When buying from a photographer, you're buying more than just an object/photograph. You are buying hundreds of hours of failures and experimentation. You are buying days, weeks and months of frustration and moments of Pure Joy. You are not just buying a thing, you're buying a piece of heart, part of a soul, a moment of someone's life. Most importantly, you're' buying artist's more time to do something they are passionate about.

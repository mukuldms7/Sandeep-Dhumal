My Journey

Being a wildlife enthusiast and an ardent nature lover, I ventured into a path that was less trodden and there was no looking back. I must admit, I initially began photography as a hobby in my spare time, however somewhere down the lane, there was a restlessness that developed into a passion to conserve the rich yet disappearing heritage that India is blessed with.

Having travelled extensively throughout India the past ten years risking life and limb, it was through photography that I wanted to create and spread awareness and contribute to their protection and conservation. Keeping this in mind, me along with other committed individuals, have initiated ‘OREKA’ Foundation - which solely caters to conserving the little known endangered species of flora and fauna of India.
